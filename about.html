Who I Am

I’m building experience through hands-on practice, home lab learning, and real-world problem solving. My goal is to join a team where I can grow into a skilled help desk / IT support technician while continuing to provide great service to clients who need reliable tech help.

  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi problems, basic IP/DNS troubleshooting, “can’t reach internal resources” basics
  • Printers & peripherals: setup, drivers, stuck queues, and common printing issues

How I Work

  1. Listen and clarify. I ask a few questions to understand the issue and urgency.
  2. Diagnose methodically. Start with the simplest checks, then dig deeper if needed.
  3. Fix + prevent. Resolve the problem and explain how to avoid it next time.
  4. Document. I keep short, clear notes so solutions are repeatable.
