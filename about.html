Who I Am

I provide straightforward tech support and device repair for people who just want their technology working again. I focus on clear communication, honest recommendations, and solutions that make sense for your situation.

What I Do

  • PC and laptop troubleshooting (slow performance, errors, startup issues)
  • Printer setup and fixes
  • Wi-Fi and basic network troubleshooting
  • Software installs, updates, and driver issues
  • Device setup and consultations (new computer, upgrades, backups)
  • Remote support for many software-related problems

How I Work

  1. Listen first. I’ll ask a few questions to understand what’s happening.
  2. Diagnose. I’ll explain what I find in plain language.
  3. Fix and prevent. I’ll repair the issue and show you simple ways to avoid it again.

What You Can Expect

  • Clear, patient explanations
  • Respect for your time and your data
  • Honest guidance (including when repair isn’t worth it)
  • Simple documentation of what was done
